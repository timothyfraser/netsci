SYSEN 5470 · Instructor · Sketch ↔ LC

Pedagogical
Wiring Diagram.

Every sketchpad prompt is engineered to set up a specific Learning Check. Sketch first, code second. This one-pager makes the alignment explicit — useful when adjusting a sketch and you want to know which LC depends on it.

Sketch Skill Primes Learning Check The hinge
Sketch 01 — Place nodes, draw edges12 min Identify Build a Network LC Forces students to defend node-and-edge choices before any code can hide ambiguity.
Sketch 02 — Two tables, one network10 min Identify Joins LC Reveals the silent-column-collision failure mode — drawn first, debugged later.
Sketch 03a — Aggregate by attribute10 min Identify Aggregation LC (first half) Makes the diagonal-stickiness pattern visible before it's a number.
Sketch 03b — Two competing aggregations10 min Identify Aggregation LC (second half) Shows that "the right resolution" depends on the question, not the data.
Sketch 04 — Bridges vs hubs15 min Measure Centrality LC Most expensive sketch — students have to argue from the question to the metric.
Sketch 05 — Plan an attack12 min Measure Supply Chain LC Targets the "busiest first" heuristic before the lab disproves it.
Sketch 06 — Find the modules10 min Measure DSM Clustering LC Students try to draw module boundaries — they realize there's not a unique answer.
Sketch 07 — Permute the labels15 min Infer Permutation LC Hard sketch — sets up the unblocked-vs-blocked-null distinction by hand.
Sketch 08 — Sample the network12 min Identify Sampling LC Ego- vs edge-sampling drawn side-by-side; the visual difference is the whole point.
Sketch 09 — A counterfactual12 min Predict Counterfactual MC LC "What if we added this edge?" — sketched in two states before the MC runs them 2000 times.
Sketch 10 — Message-pass by hand18 min Predict GNN by Hand LC The most concrete sketch in the course — one round of GNN propagation done on paper.
Sketch 11 — Predict from position15 min Predict GNN + XGBoost LC Surfaces the question "does network position add information beyond raw features?"
