Network & data

  • 437 nodes · 18,402 edges
  • Node = a bike station
  • Edge = trips A→B, May 2026
  • Weight = trip count · directed
  • Density 0.097 · one component
  • Filtered to ≥30 trips/month

Method & findings

  • Degree vs betweenness vs random
  • Only 4 of 10 stations overlap
  • Eigenvector ≈ degree (ρ .88), ≠ betweenness (.41)
  • Betweenness vs degree: p < 0.001
  • Null model: critical 6% by chance (95% CI 3–9%)
Key finding
Busy ≠ load-bearing. The cut points are low-traffic bridges, not the busiest hubs — "protect the busiest first" guards the wrong stations.
