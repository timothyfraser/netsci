SYSEN 5470 · Help · AI Use Policy

AI in This Course.

Short version: AI is welcome for syntax, not for thinking. The longer version lives below. When in doubt, ask.

🎯The principle

AI can accelerate your syntax. The course is about your thinking. Project reports and reflections are how we see your thinking — so they must be in your own words.

✅You may use AI for

  • Syntax help and debugging code
  • Clarifying questions about concepts in readings or labs
  • Explaining error messages
  • Generating example data for practice

🚫You may not use AI for

  • Writing your project reports (the prose must be your own)
  • Answering Learning Check questions for you
  • Writing reflections or interpretation prompts
💬

The Course Companion (NotebookLM)

The Course Companion is grounded in this course's own materials — the glossary, the lab walkthroughs, the syllabus, the sketches. It will not write your reports for you, by design. Tap the green bubble in the bottom-right of any page to open it, or go directly: NotebookLM →

📨If in doubt, ask

Email tmf77@cornell.edu. The honest version of "is this AI use OK?" is always better than a guess.
