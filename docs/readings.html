SYSEN 5470 · Readings

Read One Thing.
Then One More.

Every case study pairs a textbook anchor (one chapter, no more) with a single engineering application paper. The textbook makes the concept rigorous; the paper makes it real. Optional readings extend either direction.

Anchor Texts

📘 Barabási, A.-L. (2016). Network Science.

Our main textbook. Free online, chapter-by-chapter. Cited in every unit.

Read at networksciencebook.com

📗 Easley & Kleinberg (2010). Networks, Crowds, & Markets.

Cornell-grown. Free PDF. Used selectively for ties, betweenness, cascades, and matching markets.

Read the PDF

📙 Menczer, Fortunato & Davis (2020). A First Course in Network Science.

Cambridge UP. A clean second-opinion textbook for students who want a different voice on the same ideas.

Companion site

📕 Bertsekas. Network Optimization.

For the routing & optimization unit. Free PDF from MIT.

Read the PDF
